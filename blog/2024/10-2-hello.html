Hello everyone and welcome to my site!

Why did I create a site you may ask? Well I'll tell you, ever since I was younger I've always had a facination about the old web, it was charming and awe-inspiring but ever since then the internet has gotten well... stale. Earlier this year I stumbled upon my friend Yui's (aka ZPTR) website and this reignited my love for these old websites, and as an homage to it I have decided to start my own.

But then I realized wait... how the fuck do you create a website in the first place. First thing I found out I needed was a domain, and after a not too long search I found here, neocities (nekoweb is better), the holy grail. Second, I needed to figure out how to code html... which I still haven't really done. But I digress, my point is I guess anyone can make a website. Okay, so I have like one more minute to write the rest of this so as an ending I just would like to mention some people who inspired me and some cool websites to visit.
